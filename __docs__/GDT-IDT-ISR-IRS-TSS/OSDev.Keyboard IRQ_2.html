Use this, it will remap the pics to interrupt on 0x20->0x2F, and mask off everything but IRQ 0 and IRQ 1. You can turn your interrupts back on then and see what happens.

Code:
      MOV AL, 0x11
      OUT 0x20, AL
      OUT 0xA0, AL

      MOV AL, 0x20
      OUT 0x21, AL
      ADD AL, 0x08
      OUT 0xA1, AL

      MOV AL, 0x04
      OUT 0x21, AL
      MOV AL, 0x02
      OUT 0xA1, AL

      MOV AL, 0x01
      OUT 0x21, AL
      MOV AL, 0x01
      OUT 0xA1, AL

      MOV AL, 0xFC
      OUT 0x21, AL
      MOV AL, 0xFF
      OUT 0xA1, AL
